Registered Innovators
Select the type of report you are submitting today
Every Month

Monthly Check-In

Your regular progress update. 40 questions covering your current stage, users, phase progress, finances, and responsible AI compliance. Takes 30 to 45 minutes.

Every 3 Months

Quarterly Review

All monthly questions plus a financial statement, one full user case study, a partnership status table, and an analytics export. Takes 2 to 3 hours.

When a Phase Is Complete

Milestone Report

Submit when you have completed a grant phase. Covers completion evidence, third-party validation, lessons learned, and a go/no-go decision for the next phase. Takes 1 to 2 hours.

End of Grant Period

End of Grant Report

The comprehensive final evaluation covering all outcomes vs objectives, full KPI results, financial reconciliation, sustainability evidence, and community impact. Takes 3 to 4 hours.
